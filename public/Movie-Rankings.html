Movie #2: V For Vendetta

V for Vendetta is related more to some political matters that are taking place in the United Kingdom. This man, who seemed to have been a prisoner because of his skin, actions, sexuality is trying to overthrow the government in England. He is getting the attention of a young journalist and he is trying in some sort of inhumane way to make her realize that the government was doing all she went through to a lot of people. The suspense, the drama, and the story of this movie makes you wonder if in all the countries the government is taking similar actions towards people who are different from what the majority of the population is used to, which we like to call as the norm.
